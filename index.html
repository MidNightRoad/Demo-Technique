History Of The Royal Family

From 1603, when Scottish King James VI inherited the throne of England as James I, both kingdoms were ruled by a single monarch.
From 1649 to 1660, the monarchical tradition was broken by the Republicans of the Commonwealth of England, following the Wars of the Three Kingdoms.

One of them was the Kingdom of Colchis.
